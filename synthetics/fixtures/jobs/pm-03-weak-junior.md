**Location:** Austin, TX (On-site required)
**Salary:** $75,000 - $95,000

StartupXYZ is an early-stage consumer app startup building the next generation of social shopping. We're a small team of 15 people looking to make our first product hire.

## The Role

We're looking for an Associate Product Manager to join as our first PM hire. You'll work directly with our founders to help define and execute our product vision. This is an entry-level role ideal for someone early in their PM career.

- Assist founders in documenting product requirements

- Manage our product backlog in Notion
- Coordinate between design and engineering
- Help plan and execute product launches
- Track key metrics and create weekly reports

## What We're Looking For

- 0-2 years of product management experience
- Bachelor's degree in any field
- Strong organizational and communication skills
- Basic familiarity with Figma or similar tools
- Interest in consumer apps and social commerce
- Comfortable with ambiguity and fast-paced environments
- Located in Austin or willing to relocate

## Nice to Have

- Experience with SQL or basic data analysis
- Background in design or engineering
- Personal interest in fashion or e-commerce

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary for an entry-level role
- Equity grant with growth potential
- Health insurance coverage
- Startup experience with direct founder mentorship
- Austin office with snacks and weekly team lunches
- Opportunity to grow into a senior role

## Why This Isn't For Everyone

This is an entry-level position with limited resources. Senior PMs looking for established processes and large teams should look elsewhere. We're looking for someone scrappy who wants to learn.

Apply if you're excited about joining an early-stage startup and growing your PM skills from the ground up!
