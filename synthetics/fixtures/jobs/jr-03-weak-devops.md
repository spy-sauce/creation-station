# Junior DevOps Engineer

**Company:** CloudScale Solutions
**Location:** Chicago, IL (On-site required)
**Salary:** $75,000 - $95,000

## About CloudScale

CloudScale Solutions is a managed services provider helping mid-market companies migrate to and operate in the cloud. We manage infrastructure for over 200 clients across AWS, GCP, and Azure.

## Role Overview

We're seeking a Junior DevOps Engineer to join our infrastructure team. You'll focus on maintaining client cloud environments, automating deployments, and ensuring 24/7 system reliability. This is a hands-on role for someone passionate about infrastructure and operations.

## Key Responsibilities

- Monitor and maintain production Kubernetes clusters
- Write Terraform and Ansible scripts for infrastructure automation
- Respond to production incidents and participate in on-call rotation
- Configure and manage CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins and GitLab CI
- Implement security best practices and compliance requirements
- Document infrastructure changes and runbooks
- Assist with client onboarding and migrations

## Requirements

- 1-2 years of experience in DevOps, SRE, or systems administration
- Strong Linux administration skills (Ubuntu, CentOS)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Familiarity with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Understanding of networking concepts (TCP/IP, DNS, load balancing)
- Scripting skills in Bash, Python, or Go
- AWS or GCP certification preferred

## Nice to Have

- Experience with monitoring tools (Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana)
- Knowledge of security tools and practices
- Database administration experience (MySQL, PostgreSQL)
- Previous MSP or consulting experience

## What We Offer

- Base salary + performance bonus
- Health and dental insurance
- 401(k) with 4% match
- Professional development budget
- On-call compensation
- Path to senior roles within 2 years

## Schedule

- Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM CT
- On-call rotation every 4 weeks
- Some weekend work during maintenance windows

This is an on-site position at our Chicago headquarters. Remote work is not available for this role.
